Empanadas

Description

Crispy fried dough with a delicious seasoned beef center.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Goya Pre-made Dough
  • Green Olives
  • Adobo Seasoning
  • Red Peppers (optional)

Steps:

  • Season with Adobo and prepare ground beef
  • Stuff pre-made disks
  • Fork edges of dough to seal
  • Fry 5-7 minutes, high heat until crispy
  • Enjoy
