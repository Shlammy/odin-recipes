Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich

Description

Bread with peanut butter and jelly, can't get much more simple than that. 😃

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps:

  • Gather ingredients, lay down 2 slices of bread
  • Evenly spread peanut butter on a single slice of bread
  • Evenly spread jelly on the other slice of bread
  • Put slices together, peanut butter side to jelly side
  • Enjoy your hand-crafted PB&J sandwich
