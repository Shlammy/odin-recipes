Pizza

Description

Baked dough with pasta sauce, cheese, and assorted toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pre-made dough
  • Pasta sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Any topping of your choice

Steps:

  • Form your dough into an even circular form
  • Spread sauce over dough, leaving a 1'' border
  • Add cheese and toppings of your choice
  • Bake at 400 degrees F until edges of dough are crispy and cheese is melted
  • Enjoy your fresh pizza
